Yunpeng Li

PhD Student (2019)

Institute of Information Engineering, CAS

He is a first-year PhD student at Institute of Information Engineering (IIE), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), supervised by Prof. Yue Hu. Before this, he got his Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from Shandong University (2015-2019).

His primary research interests are mainly in Natural Language Processing and Multi-modal Machine Learning. Specifically, his current research focuses on vision and language, e.g. relation extraction, visual dialog, and visual question answering.
